FAQ

A Phishing email is a type of internet scam in which an attacker trys to trick a victim into thinking a fraudulent email is legitimate.

CatPhishing101 works by collecting information from emails to help determine legitimacy.
We examine:
- The sender email.
- The content of the email.
We feed this information to an algorithm, which determines the likeihood of a scam email.

CatPhishing101 examines your email data, but never collects or transfers your data.

No security tool is 100% foolproof against scams.
CatPhishing101 can be a helpful tool to discover scams, but conduct your own independant assessment for validation.
